# Modes

Use these as defaults, then adapt.

## Blog Post

Target: 700-1800 words.

Suggested structure:
1. Hook with a concrete moment.
2. Context: why this mattered.
3. What happened (timeline with key decisions).
4. Lessons learned (with evidence).
5. What to copy vs what not to copy.
6. Strong close (next action or practical takeaway).

## GitHub Discussion

Target: 250-900 words.

Suggested structure:
1. Problem/context in 2-4 sentences.
2. Proposal or conclusion.
3. Evidence (links, examples, data).
4. Tradeoffs / open questions.
5. Explicit ask (feedback, decision, next step).

## Internal Note / Memo

Target: 300-1200 words.

Suggested structure:
1. Why now.
2. Current state.
3. Decision and rationale.
4. Risks + mitigations.
5. Owners and next milestones.

## Choosing narrative weight

- **Low narrative:** mostly technical; minimal anecdotes.
- **Medium narrative:** one personal moment + clear technical throughline.
- **High narrative:** scene-led opening and multiple story beats, while keeping claims specific.
